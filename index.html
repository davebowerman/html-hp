Discipleship with Results

Freedom from destructive habitual behavior that pulls you in like a Sci-fi "tractor beam," causing you to betray your dreams and aspirations, and destroy the trust of those who believe in you.

Power that enables you to operate from a clear head and allows you to live with faith, hope, and love, thereby making better decisions that bring greater achievement.

Purpose that brings about a greater sense of identity because you're living a truly transformed life rather than just trying not to do the wrong things.

Based on the Book

Higher Pursuits, authored by Dave Bowerman, is a discipleship approach that leads us to live with freedom, power, and purpose by helping us recognize seven self-deceptions that hold us back and seven higher pursuits that lead us into the experience of God.

As a man, our coaching brings you into a spiritual way to approach personal transformation, rather than a psychological or behavioral program for change. However, as a result, as Jesus put it, you will "know the truth," which in turn will set you free, psychologically and behaviorally. You will find inner strength and outer success, and wonder it took so long.
